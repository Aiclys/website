How to set up mpd with mpc and ncmpcpp April 12, 2024

Intro Why should you use mpd, mpc and ncmpcpp and what are they? Mpd is a minimalistic but powerful music client/daemon that is used as backend in a lot of different apps for playing music. Managing it without any frontend is nearly impossible, which is why it is often used in combination with mpc for control and ncmpcpp as frontend to do things like browsing music files, choosing the song, arranging playlists etc.
